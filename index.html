The program

A program that seeks to foster the development of skills such as leadership and adaptability, increase self-esteem, and reduce anxiety through adventure. Our educational focus is on mountain sports, survival skills, and outdoor recreation activities. The complete Summer program has a duration of 6 weeks. It consists of three modules and each one is composed of two weeks.
Each week we explore a different topic.

On July 7, we begin with the Survival module, the first week’s theme being Fire Skills. The idea of ​​the themes is to have fun and work on different skills to learn how to overcome challenges and therefore be more secure and confident in our actions and how we feel.
