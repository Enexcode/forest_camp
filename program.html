The program

OBJECTIVES

Our main goal for this program is to pass on knowledge of the wilderness and survival techniques to your children in an effort to help them develop cognitive skills, leadership traits and executive function abilities.

FLORA & FAUNA

In addition to spending time inside the forest, ample opportunities to engage with various animals, both wild & domestic exist inside the park. Playing with monkeys and feeding the iguanas are a forest camper favorite! We also explore other environments such as lakes & the ocean.

PLAY

In that way, Forest Campers gain an awareness of Nature and have extensive opportunities to play outside. Ludic activities, while immersed in natural spaces, allow learning to happen effortlessly. Trough nature campers develop non linear thinking, consciousness of well-being and embrace an extensive amount of health benefits.

NUTRITION

It’s important to note that nutrition is a key component of this program as well and one of factors that differentiates this program from others. We follow Child Nutrition and Cooking standards provided by Stanford University in 2022. As a result, most foods I offer are home-made by me and processed foods are scarce. Sodas and fried foods are prohibited.

CAmpers

Designed for kids ages 7-12, this adventure-filled camp from 9am to 4pm offers the perfect blend of fun and learning in the great outdoors. Participants will enjoy thrilling mountain sports, heartwarming interactions with animals, and immersive nature-based activities at Scape Park.

Every moment is packed with excitement and discovery, fostering a deep connection with the environment while celebrating the magic of the season. Give your child the gift of exploration, fresh air, and unforgettable memories— reserve a spot today!
