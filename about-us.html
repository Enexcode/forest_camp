Meet the team

At Forest Camp, we boast a highly diverse and passionate team, all set and ready to turn your child's summer into an unforgettable adventure! Our carefully selected professionals come equipped with expertise in neuro-education, outdoor sports, wilderness first aid, and forest skills—ensuring that every camper receives the attention and nurturing they deserve.

Whether it's scaling a rock wall, navigating a forest trail, or learning vital survival skills, our team is committed to creating lasting memories. We believe that every child should feel empowered, confident, and connected to the great outdoors. Join us at Forest Camp, where every day is a new chapter of adventure and growth in the wild!
